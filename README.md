# Volery

### Your suite. Your AI.

**A private AI that lives on your devices — not in someone else's cloud.**

</div>

---

Volery runs a capable AI **on your phone**. No account, no sign-up, and by default nothing you
type ever leaves the device. When you *do* want a bigger model, you plug in your own — Volery
never routes your data through us.

The agents are birds gathered in a *volery* (a flock):

- 🐦 **Scout** — the on-device model. Free, offline, private. Runs entirely on your phone.
- ☁️ **Sage** — a bigger cloud model you bring yourself (OpenAI / OpenRouter / Groq / any
  OpenAI-compatible endpoint), or **your own computer** over your private network. Your key,
  your provider — the data goes where *you* point it.

## Features

- **On-device chat** — ask, translate, summarize, explain, all without a connection.
- **Bring your own model** — load `.gguf` (llama.cpp) or `.litertlm` models, or download one
  in-app. Pick the size that fits your phone.
- **On-device tools** — Scout can use the time, calculator, battery/device info, flashlight,
  calendar, contacts, location, clipboard, notes, alarms and maps directions — all handled on
  the phone. It can also draft a text, an email, or start a call, pre-filled for you to confirm.
- **Voice** — dictate with the mic, or hold to record a **voice note** and let Volery turn it
  into a clean, structured Markdown report (great for lectures and meetings).
- **Web tools** *(optional)* — web search, Wikipedia, weather, news, crypto prices, read-a-link.
- **Yours to shape** — themes, accent colours, custom assistant persona, chat appearance.

## Privacy

- **Private by default.** Scout runs 100% on-device; your messages and the tools' data
  (calendar, contacts, location, notes, voice) stay on your phone.
- **Cloud only when you choose it.** The only thing that ever leaves the device is what you
  explicitly send to **your own** Sage provider. Speech recognition prefers the on-device engine.
- No analytics-driven profiles. See the [privacy policy](https://azfarh95.github.io/volery-legal/).
